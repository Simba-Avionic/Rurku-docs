\section{Purpose of this document}

Without written requirements, goals drift quietly (1\,km $\rightarrow$ 3\,km,
ethanol $\rightarrow$ RP-1, parachute $\rightarrow$ powered landing). The SRD
is the reference for major design decisions.

\noindent\textbf{Audience:} department leads (Engine, GSE, Recovery, Aerostructure,
Avionics, Simulations) and anyone proposing a design change that affects
performance, mass, safety, or mission success.

\noindent\textbf{Out of scope:} pad timelines and abort scripts (CONOPS);
block diagrams and interface details (SAD); test procedures (Test Plan).
