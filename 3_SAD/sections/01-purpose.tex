\section{Purpose of this document}

Without a shared architecture map, every department invents its own picture of
``the rocket'': who owns the valves, where power ends, what mates to the airframe.
The SAD is the design-level map so new people and other subsystems can see the
whole system quickly.

\noindent\textbf{Audience:} department leads (Engine, GSE, Recovery, Aerostructure,
Avionics, Simulations) and anyone changing a boundary between subsystems.

\noindent\textbf{Out of scope:} measurable Must/Should tables (SRD); pad timelines,
roles, and abort scripts (CONOPS); test procedures and results (TPTR); full
connector pinouts and protocol specs (ICD --- planned).
