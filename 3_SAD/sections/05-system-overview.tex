\noindent\begin{tabularx}{\linewidth}{@{}l X X@{}}
\toprule

\midrule
PROP & Thrust generation, ignition, engine MEOP hardware &
  Pad fueling procedures (CONOPS); thrust \emph{requirement} (SRD) \\
FEED & Propellant / pressurant storage and delivery to PROP &
  Engine injector design internals \\
STR & Structural load path, OML, integration mounts &
  Propellant chemistry \\
AV & Onboard compute, sensing, power, RF link &
  Pad radio net design details (may share with GSE) \\
REC & Safe descent and locate-ability &
  Flight trajectory design (SIM / SRD) \\
GSE & Safe remote fueling, valve actuation, pad DAQ &
  Onboard flight events after disconnect \\
