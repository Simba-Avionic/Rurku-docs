\section{Purpose of this document}

After a year of work, most real project knowledge lives in the test history
--- not in design assumptions. This TPTR captures that history.

\noindent\textbf{Question it answers:} \emph{What are we checking, and what did
we learn?}

\noindent\textbf{Audience:} people running the test, department leads, and
anyone deciding go/no-go for the next CONOPS stage.

\noindent\textbf{Rule:} there is no ``test without paper''. Even a quick check
gets a short record, or in a month nobody will know what happened.
